Refund Policy

Refunds will be granted only in the event of cancellation or rescheduling of the festival. If the festival is cancelled in its entirety, ticket holders will be refunded the face value of their ticket. If the festival is rescheduled, ticket holders will have the option to either use their ticket for the rescheduled date or receive a refund.

Refunds will not be granted for any other reason. All sales are final and non-transferable. In the event of a cancellation or rescheduling, ticket holders will be notified via the email address provided at the time of purchase.

We strongly recommend that all ticket holders purchase ticket insurance or consider purchasing refundable travel arrangements to protect against unforeseen circumstances. The festival is not responsible for any expenses incurred by ticket holders in connection with the festival, including but not limited to travel, lodging, or other expenses.
